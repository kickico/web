KickCoin. A solid currency, not just a token

KickCoin is a virtual currency that will be accepted and used by the projects that are launched on our platform (as well as by all our partners) and later accepted as a payment method too. KC will be primarely used as a payment method on our platform.

We expect the following growth rate for KickCoin

Since KICKICO takes half the commission of ICO campaigns in their tokens, the positive growth of these cryptocurrencies affects the entire portfolio of the platform and, thus, indirectly supports the value of KC.

In addition to that, the value of KickCoin tokens will also be supported by creation of a token fund: KICKICO takes 4% of the tokens or cryptocurrency produced by an ICO campaign and deposits it in the fund. This fund of campaign tokens will back the value of KC tokens, as projects launched through KICKICO grow in value.

KickCoin activates Bancor protocol and, being a "Smart token", will have reserves that makes it liquid within Bancor network.

KickCoin will also be a Network token for all projects raising funds on KICKICO platform. This means that KickCoin's value will be supported by all KICKICO projects' tokens, that make reserves within Bancor Network. This will create an arbitrage opportunity to bring KickCoin's value higher on the exchanges

This economy model is called the KICKONOMY

During the first two years after the ICO we will make a limited bonus emission to reward our community members who hold KickCoins. The bonuses will be credited on the last day of every month.
