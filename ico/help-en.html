Step-by-step guide to join KICKICO crowdsale

(even if you don’t own any crypto assets and you never used crypto before)

 

Buy Bitcoins on Localbitcoins.net

1. Go to Localbitcoins.net, and fill out the registration form, if you have an account already - skip this step and move to the next one.

If you want to switch interface language - you can find language switch at the bottom of the page.

Check your email after completing the registration and confirm your email. After the confirmation - you will be redirected to the page with detailed instructions.

2. Go to the main page and in the search box fill in the form with the amount you want to buy in your currency, your location and choose a payment method. If you're unsure how you want to pay, choose "All online offers" as your payment method. The site will list bitcoin traders available in your region.

3. From the list of advertisements, choose one from a trader with a good reputation score and a high amount of trades. The response time indicator shows if a trader replies within five minutes (green), within 30 minutes (yellow) or slower than 30 minutes (grey). You can click the 'Buy' button to view more information about an advertisement.

4. After you press the 'Buy' button you'll see more information about the advertisement, including the terms of the trade. Read through them before submitting the trade request, if you don't agree with them you can go back to the previous page and choose another advertisement.

To start the trade, type in the blue box how much you want to buy, enter a message for the seller and click the Send trade request button to the start the trade. Be sure you're ready to pay when clicking the button, if you don't pay before the payment window is over, the trade will be automatically cancelled.

5. Once you have made the payment, click the I have paid button. Once the trader has verified that your payment has been received your Bitcoin will be released from escrow and they are instantly available in your LocalBitcoins wallet.

And that's all there is to it, congratulations on your first Bitcoin trade!

Send your Bitcoins to an exchange

You may choose from variety of exchanges to send your Bitcoins to.

This step is needed to exchange your Bitcoins to Ethereum, so that you will be able to send Ethereum to your private wallet and then to KICKICO crowdsale.

In this very guide we chose EXMO as a service to exchange Bitcoins to Ethereum allowing withdrawal up to 500 ETH with no ID verification. But all other Exchanges actually do the same thing - you transfer them Bitcoins and exchange to Ethereum.

1. Go to https://exmo.me and sign up (check your email for activation link).

* In case you can’t access Exmo exchange - you can download Exodus.io wallet and send your Bitcoins to Exodus wallet and exchange them into Ethereum (but it may be longer and the rate might be higher). Exodus is a great wallet to store your crypto-assets. Just download and install it - and you can use it. Go to Wallet and select Bitcoin - then click Receive button and you will see your Exodus Bitcoin address. Send your Bitcoins to this address. After you received your Bitcoins - go to exchange tab and exchange Bitcoins to Ethereum. The exchange process may take up to 1 hour or more. After you received Ethereum - you can send it from your wallet: go to Wallet, and select Ethereum, then click Send button, insert address and amount and click send.

* Another alternative to an exchange is Changelly service. This service allows you to buy Ethereum directly using your bank card. The interface is really intuitive, BUT LOOK FOR THE RATE. The rate is often really high, so use it in carefully. It may even ask for ID verification process and so on.

2. On Exmo.me choose a Wallet section in the top menu. Then press «Deposit» for BTC.

3. Create a Bitcoin wallet.

4. The address of the wallet for transferring the bitcoins to recharge the balance appears. Copy it.

5. Go back to Localbitcoins.net select «Wallet», Send Bitcoins tab. Enter the Receiving bitcoin address and the Amount in bitcoins and click continue. Next screen will ask your account password to confirm the transaction. Enter the password and confirm.

6. Wait for the network to process your payment and your balance will be added to the stock exchange. Transfer of funds to the balance of Exmo.me pass through 6 network confirmations (6 confirmations). Check the progress of the transaction on the link that the bot offers you.

 

Exchange Bitcoins for Ethereum(ETH)

1. Go to Exmo.me in the «exchange» section and choose in the drop-down list what you give to BTC and get ETH.

2. Enter the amount in the bitcoins you want to change and click the «exchange». Now you have ETH on the balance sheet.

 

Create Ethereum wallet on myetherwallet.com

1. Go to https://myetherwallet.com. And follow the instructions to create your wallet ether.

* In case you are using Exodus wallet - you don’t need to create the address. Use the private key from the Exodus wallet to access your Ethereum address via myetherwallet.com:

— Export a private key: if you are on macOS, the develop menu is under 'Exodus'. If you are using Windows or Linux, it can be accessed by pressing Ctrl+Shift+D.

Select Developer > Assets > Ethereum > Export private keys

Go to the folder and open the export file. You will see csv file - open it and find you private key (the sybmols after the last comma), copy it.

— Then go to https://myetherwallet.com and select Send Ether & Tokens. Enter your wallet using the Private Key

2. Save your file and click the button that you understand. Continue.

3. On the next screen save your private key and click on the save your address button.

4. On the next screen choose How do you want to access your wallet. Select the Keystore File (UTC / JSON) and click on the button on the right. Select the file that you saved in step 21 and enter the password for access.

5. After unlocking the address you will see below your wallet number.

 

Send Ethereum to your MEW address

Go to Exmo.me. In the Wallet section opposite ETH, select «withdraw» and in the next window enter the amount available in ETH and the address of your ETH wallet from myetherwallet.com and press «withdraw».

 

Send Ethereum to KICKICO crowdsale

1. Having received ETH on your wallet https://myetherwallet.com, choose «Send Ether & Tokens» in the menu. Unlock your wallet with the JSON file and your password.

2. Enter the address and amount of the transfer. Set gas limit to 300000 and Gas price to 21, click generate the transaction.

You can find the address for sending ETH on the website:

https://www.kickico.com/ru/campaigns/999/ico

Select “Contribute” and follow instructions until you see ETH address for joining crowdsale. Copy the address and send ETH using myetherwallet.com
